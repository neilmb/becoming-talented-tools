--- layout: base.njk title: Becoming Talented exercise tools ---

The book Becoming Talented: A Systematic Method for the Development of Ear Training and Music Reading Skills ([amazon link](https://www.amazon.com/Becoming-Talented-Systematic-Development-Training/dp/B0CW6HHF7L)) has a number of exercises that require various creations of musical notes or sequences of musical intervals.

This site collects some web-based tools for making it easier to perform these exercises.

  • Exercise 1.2: White-Key Interval Cycles
  • Exercise 1.3: Directed White-Key Tracking
  • Exercise 1.5: Tracking line interval identification
  • Exercise 1.9: Half-step Interval Cycles
  • Exercise 1.10: Directed Half-step Interval Tracking
  • Exercise 3.1: Vertical identification over a drone
  • Exercise 3.2: Ploger harmonic interval drills
  • Exercise 3.3: Ploger melodic interval drills
